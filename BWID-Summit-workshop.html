BWID Summit R Workshop

Welcome to the Black Women in Data Summit R workshop!

In this workshop you’ll get started with R, RStudio and the tidyverse. This is a hands on workshop, so you’ll have the opportunity to try things out!

This is a short version of a Carpentries workshop. Learn more about The Carpentries and their workshops, lessons and instructor training.
